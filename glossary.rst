Glossary
==========

.. glossary::

    existing client
        A client (company) who had successfully started a job (with or without trial) before having another HPCJ.

    HPCJ
        *Has Posted Claimed Job*. It designates a job posted by a client and claimed by some member of
        the matchers team (the jobs record has claimer_id and claimed_at assigned).

    failed trial
        Customers usually opt for two weeks trial period to see if they can work with
        chosen talent. The period is called *a trial*. If the client decides not to work with a talent
        the trial is called *a failed trial*.

    no show
        An event when a prospect didn't show up for an arranged call.

    pathetic lead
        The Lead that didn't finish entry survey when applied.
